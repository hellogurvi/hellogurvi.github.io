Smart Simran - app

This app reminds users to do Simran (meditation) throughout the day. The user receives a Gurbani quote and the ability to log their Simran in a diary. Also the user can look at their history and can compare thier Simran and phone usage with other users of the app. All data is recorded anonymously.
