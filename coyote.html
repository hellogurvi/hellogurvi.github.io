COYOTE RENTALS

Based in Kamploops, Cayote Rentals helps both tenants and landlords come together as a online community and build a reputation for themselves. The tenant user can search for properties and see the landlords profile and rating. The landlord user can accept or decline a tenants offer for their property. The landlord user can view potential tenant profiles to rent their property based on their rating.
